Detailed Description

Boomerang controller.

This is essentially just a Pilons controller with an intermediate "carrot" point

The boomerang controller is not magic, and it works best if you are already facing in the general direction of the point you'd like to go to As just a general rule of thumb, the target point should be in the 120 degrees in front or behind the robot. This will also likely be slower than other controllers.
